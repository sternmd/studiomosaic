Omnium Peak

Euphorm

Omnium Peak is Euphorm's third full-length album. Characteristic to previous Euphorm experiments, this 18 track compilation nods to the lo-fi ideals of ambient, blurry minimalism, and dub techno, along with newer elements of drone. A polyrhythmic collection of ghosts and dreams.


Genre: Ambient / Experimental / Minimal / Drone

Album Art: Maxwell Stern

Release: April 2016

Duration: 1:41:09
