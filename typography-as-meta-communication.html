Typography As Meta-Communication

Studio Mosaic’s submission for the Society of Typographic Arts Jack Weiss Scholarship: Typography as Meta-Communication. We incorporated images including Aristotle, the major philosophical figure behind metaphysics, as well as a pixelated rainforest to describe the prompt’s “..meta is dense like a jungle.” The subdued receipt-like copy represent our fleeting emotional insecurities about designing the work itself. This refers to our designers’ internal mind-chatter, prices of the reward money, and names of the judges themselves. Poster available for purchase upon contact.
