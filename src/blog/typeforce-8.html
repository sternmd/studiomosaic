Typeforce 8

Every year, curators Ed Marzsewski & Dawn Hancock select the works to be installed and exhibited in Chicago’s Typeforce. Historically, these works have comprised some of the best type or lettering-based art being created in our city and beyond. Typeforce premieres its eighth annual exhibition showcasing Chicago's emerging typographic artists including Adé Hogue, Crystal Zapata, Sky White, and Tyler Deal. Opening reception Fri 2/17, 6-11 PM. The show runs through March 4, 2017.
