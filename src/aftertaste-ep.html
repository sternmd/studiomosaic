Aftertaste EP

Euphorm

Aftertaste EP is a mini-collection of heavy techno loops produced by Euphorm over the course of sleepless night sessions. This obscure electronic project covers experiments in genres such as dub techno, minimal techno, and more. A pulsing, lo-fi techno apocalypse.

Genre: Dub Techno, Minimal Techno

Album Art: Maxwell Stern

Release: Dec 2014

Duration: 54:35
