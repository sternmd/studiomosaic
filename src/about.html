Our Studio

Studio Mosaic is an independent artist-run collective in Chicago. Our self-initiated projects cover a variety of disciplines including music, printed matter, graphic design, and art direction. Our goal is to craft a space to freely collaborate, influence, and engage. We think of Studio Mosaic as one big art project and a foster home for nourishing a creative lifestyle. Above all, art is meant be shared.

Our Work

Our works are inspired by the collaborative DIY efforts of our in-house members:

  • Music albums & mixes
  • Limited edition prints
  • Short-run art books
  • Digital experiments
  • Live events
